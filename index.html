Lab Calculators

A small collection of browser-based calculators for optics and cold-molecule experiments. These tools run locally in the browser and do not need installation.

Wavelength and frequency conversion for the LiRb STIRAP SFG laser system.

ABCD-matrix Gaussian beam propagation with lenses, plots, and CSV save/load.

Basic Lab Conversions

Open →

Quick conversions for dBm, RF voltage, optical frequency, wavelength, cm⁻¹, photon energy, temperature, and Gaussian beam intensity.
